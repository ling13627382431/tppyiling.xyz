About The Game

Monster Survivors throws you into a high-stakes arena where endless waves of menacing creatures close in relentlessly. Your goal is straightforward but deeply challenging: survive as long as possible. Every run presents a new challenge, forcing you to adapt your strategy and forge powerful skill combinations to annihilate the monster legions.
